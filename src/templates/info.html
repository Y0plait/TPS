TPS is a python movie researcher. A kind of torrent search engine.
It is extremly simple go on the search page

  • Type the name of your movie in the blank form
  • Click on search
  • And here you go !! A list with all the results with their name, magnet link, size and even number of seeders !

Features

  • Choose the language of the movie (currently only French and English are available).
  • Choose the quality of the movie.
  • Automatically download your movie by simply clicking the little ‚ûï button next to its number of seeders.
  • Export the output to a csv.
